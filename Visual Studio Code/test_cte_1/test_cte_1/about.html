WELCOME TO THE ABOUT PAGE OF THE SITE

The site was created to simulate a tic-tac-toe game.

Account has to be created to play the game. The score of the player is kept in records. A win means +1 point, loss is -1 and a draw does not change the score.

The Game can be played in two ways:

Multi-Player mode - Here the person needs to have another human present by his/her side. The player logs into his/her accont, and only the score of the same is kept track of. The other player who accompanies the account-holder in concern will not have his/her updated even if he/she has an account. The account-holder player makes the first move and the game proceeds so on.

Computer mode - Here the players logs into his/her account and plays against the comptuer AI. The compter has been modelled "perfect". Here, perfect refers to resorting to the set of moves which always ends in either a draw, or loss for the player.

During the course of the journey, the player has the option to reset his/her score. This will reinitialize his/her score to zero, irrespective of the previous value.
