  • PLAYING AGAINST ANOTHER HUMAN
    1. Have an opponent by your side for the war
    2. Whosever account is in play, gets to make the first move
    3. Whosever account is in play, only his/her score will be updated
    4. Fair-play is decided by you. Don't let your opponent make your move
    5. DO NOT RESORT TO VIOLENT/PHYSICAL METHODS TO WIN THIS WAR

 

  • PLAYING AGAINST THE COMPUTER
    1. There are no rules. YOU JUST LOSE!
